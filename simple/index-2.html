Welcome

My name is Harold Gonzales, I am a Matrix Educator residing in the beautiful city of Calgary. Being able to share the stage with the same stylist and barbers that I grew up inspiring to be like, is a blessing. There's a sense of achievement when your inspirational leaders are now learning of you and asking you questions. The path to success is to love what you do, be humble and share the creativity with others.
